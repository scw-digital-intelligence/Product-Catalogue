SCW's Analytics department provide you with data, analytics tools, modelling and intelligence to support business decisions. We provide data analysis to manage a system and the partners within it, assess how they are performing, and use population health management processes to achieve overall system efficiencies. Our analytics and intelligence services enable a shared understanding to deliver evidence-based intelligence-driven, system decision-making, using reliable accurate data and intelligence, vital to making the best decisions for patients and populations.

The Product Catalogue demonstrates all of SCW's Enterprise Insights Products managed by the Analytics Department. It brings together an array of products and tools to support commissioning, patient care, and performance monitoring. Having reliable, accurate data, and intelligence are vital to make the best decisions for patients and populations. This is true whether addressing operational, financial or contractual challenges, or working across systems to deliver population health management and improved patient experience.

Our products provide secure data and reporting, enabling monitoring of activity performance, to improve the quality and outcomes for healthcare, and deliver value for money. Each product is supported by a product owner who is familiar with the report and is involved in the design. They can provide advice and capture any further design requirements for future iteration of the report. Should you wish to discuss any reports please fill in the request form and we will put you in touch with the Product Owner.
